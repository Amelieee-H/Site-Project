M44 Mess Kit

This article takes a look at an item from my personal collection, a 1971 Swedish M44 aluminum mess kit. A mainstay of any army, rations play a key role in day to day life in any conflict, the M44 mess kit not only allows for the preparation of rations provided, but also provides facilities for basic field cooking. Explore the genius design as well as its vast functionality.
